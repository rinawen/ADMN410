About us

History: Durham House Pet was found in 2008, in the cold winter, many stray cats and dogs were taken in Miss Q's home to provide them with a place to escape from the cold. After that, for helping these stray animals, Miss Q opened a small animal shelter and named it “Love House”, which was the predecessor of Durham House Pet


Why use us: Our DHP give pets the most meticulous care, with the most professional attitude for each pet to create an exclusive plan.
